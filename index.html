This is the webpage of the Square Kilometre Array cosmological simulation focus group. The idea of this page is to provide the community with a common place to search for numerical codes or data from numerical simulations related to cosmology with the SKA.

The data is mostly stored in a server at the Flatiron Institute in New York. In this page we provide links to the location where the data can be downloaded. We also give links and host codes that carry out different calculations.

We have a wishlist to keep track of the simulation needs of the different groups, the status and the people working on them. Anyone has rights to edit this page. If you do not have, please write us.
